Last Updated 15/4/2025
At Chatrix, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website (www.chatrix.in) and services.

We collect various types of information, including:
